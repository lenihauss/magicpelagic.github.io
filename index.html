About Me

Welcome! I am Leni Hauss, a passionate biological oceanographer and pelagic ecologist with a focus on understanding the dynamics of oceanic ecosystems. My work combines field obeservations, experiments and modelling to explore the intricate interactions within the ocean's pelagic zone.
